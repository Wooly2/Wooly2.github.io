Facts About Games

Minecraft

Minecraft was created by Markus Persson and released in 2011.

Minecraft is one of the best-selling video games of all time, with over 200 million copies sold.

Roblox

Roblox was created by David Baszucki and Erik Cassel and released in 2006.

Roblox has a large and active user base, with millions of players creating and sharing their own games.

VRChat

VRChat is a virtual reality social platform that allows users to interact with others in virtual worlds.

VRChat gained popularity for its user-generated content and the ability to create and customize avatars.

League of Legends

League of Legends, commonly known as LoL, is a popular multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game.

League of Legends is developed and published by Riot Games and has a large esports scene with professional leagues and tournaments.

Call of Duty

Call of Duty is a first-person shooter game franchise developed by various studios and published by Activision.

Call of Duty games are known for their intense multiplayer modes and cinematic single-player campaigns.

Fortnite

Fortnite, developed by Epic Games, gained widespread popularity for its battle royale mode.

Fortnite has become a cultural phenomenon with its in-game events, dances, and collaborations with celebrities.

Slither.io

Slither.io is a popular online multiplayer game where players control snakes and compete to become the longest snake.

Slither.io gained popularity for its addictive gameplay and simple mechanics.
